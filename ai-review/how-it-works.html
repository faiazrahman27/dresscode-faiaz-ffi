The flow

The system becomes simple once each layer has a role

Each step solves a specific problem: discovery, ownership, content, and repeat interaction.

Step 01
Scan the QR code
Step 01

Scan the QR code

A visitor scans the QR code attached to the garment, product, badge, or collectible item. This becomes the entry point into the digital identity experience.

Step 02
Activate ownership
Step 02

Activate ownership

If the code is meant to be claimed, the user signs in and enters the scratch code. This links the physical item to the correct account and prevents uncontrolled claiming.

Step 03
Build the public page
Step 03

Build the public page

Open codes can be edited through the page builder. Users can add visuals, links, sections, socials, and custom content to shape the live profile experience.

Step 04
Publish and share
Step 04

Publish and share

After setup, future scans open the live public page instantly. Depending on the code type, the page can be personal, official, locked, branded, or redirect-based.

Ownership, content, and insights

Ownership

Claimed with control

Scratch activation helps separate physical possession from public access. This keeps ownership more structured and harder to fake.

Content

Open or official

Open codes support user customization. Locked codes support template-based official experiences controlled by admins and brands.

Insights

Trackable interaction

Once the public page is live, scans become engagement data. This turns physical items into measurable digital touchpoints.
